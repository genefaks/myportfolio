UL Buddies

UL Buddies is a platform that helps connect people of the same interests or similar interests. It gives a wholesome experience, especially to people who find it difficult to make friends. The application helps people to find people with common interests and bond over them or find a buddy to explore new interests.

Digital Prototype - Mobile App Screen

The application is driven with the idea of meeting people of common interests. We moved to the mock-up and designing low-fidelity prototype phase.

A flow was created to guide users with navigating through the app. Users sign up and add their interests, then find people in the system who have same or similar interests and eventually meet people physically.

Users were ranked with the number of similarities and range of interests the user has and display them when being listed.
