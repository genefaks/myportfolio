3D MODELING WITH MAYA

Modeling A Glazed Doughnut

With the Maya 2022 the idea was creating a glazed doughnut. Maya is a 3D visual effects software with strong character creation, rigging, animation, and simulation tools.

How it was accomplished

1

Modeling

Creating three-dimensional objects with the software tool Maya, the Torus object was modeled into the glazed doughnut with subsequent addition of polygons and vertices.

2

Sculpting

Adding more definition to the model. The aim of sculpting was to reduce the smoothness and give the doughnut a natural look. Creating more thickness for the frost and flattening out the doughnut sides.

3

Lightning

For visibility and illumination, the point light was used to create the lightning effect on the scene. A backdrop was created to reflect the light to focus more on the scene.

4

Texture

In order to give the model a natural look, texture was applied to change the appearance of the surface of the model. Colors, transparency and reflectiveness was used to create smooth and rough edges on the model.

5

Rendering

After modeling the doughnut and satisfied with the look, rendering is done to give the model the highest quality looks, improving the lights, textures, and cameras.

6

Animation

A camera animation was used to create realism to the model. This involved setting keyframes of the camera around the glazed doughnut with a total number of 366 frames used to create the dynamic reveal.
