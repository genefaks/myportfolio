About me

I'm Genevieve Fakunle, a user interface and experience designer. Presently a masters student at the University of Limerick studying Interaction and Experience Design. I have a knack for understanding human behaviour and posing solutions to conflicts are better suited to technology. I made a career switch to Product Design after my Bachelors in International Relations and Diplomacy.  

Since career switch, I have worked as a user experience researcher at Hierographics Inc which involved systematically studying target users to collect and analyze data that will help inform the product design process.

Outside of work and study, I like to get travel. A few countries I have visited are Dubai, Ethopia, Kenya. I also enjoy partying, listening to music and creating playlists.
